## Available Plans

Repolyze offers three tiers designed to match your usage needs, from quick one-off analyses to power user workflows.

### Anonymous (Free)

Try Repolyze without creating an account.

- **Daily Analyses**: 1 repository per day (per IP)
- **Share Links**: Share analysis results with others
- **All other features**: Locked (requires sign-in)

<Info>
  Anonymous usage is tracked by IP address and resets daily at midnight UTC.
</Info>

### Free Plan

Sign up with GitHub or Google OAuth to unlock more capabilities.

- **Daily Analyses**: 3 repositories per day
- **Issue Creation**: Create GitHub issues directly from analysis
- **Export Formats**: Download as Text, Markdown, or PDF
- **Share Links**: Share analysis results with others
- **AI Insights**: ❌ Pro only
- **Data Flow Diagrams**: ❌ Pro only

<Note>
  Free accounts reset daily at midnight UTC. [Sign in](/authentication) to get started.
</Note>

### Pro Plan

Unlock advanced features and higher limits for professional use.

- **Daily Analyses**: 44 repositories per day
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Get intelligent recommendations and analysis
- **Data Flow Diagrams**: Visualize data flow through your codebase
- **Issue Creation**: Create GitHub issues directly from analysis
- **Export Formats**: Download as Text, Markdown, or PDF
- **Share Links**: Share analysis results with others
- **Priority Support**: Get help when you need it

<Warning>
  Pro subscriptions are managed through [Polar.sh](https://polar.sh). Your plan expires if payment fails.
</Warning>

## Subscription Management

Repolyze uses **Polar.sh** for secure subscription management and billing.

### How It Works

1. **Sign In**: Authenticate with GitHub or Google
2. **Upgrade**: Click "Upgrade to Pro" from your dashboard
3. **Checkout**: Complete payment through Polar.sh
4. **Instant Access**: Pro features unlock immediately

### Plan Details

- Subscriptions are tied to your authenticated account
- Daily limits reset at **00:00 UTC** each day
- Usage is tracked per user (not per IP) for authenticated accounts
- Pro plan expires if payment fails (automatically downgrades to Free)

<Info>
  Your `plan` field is stored in the database and checked on every analysis request. See `schema.prisma:24` for implementation details.
</Info>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my daily limit?">
    You'll receive a rate limit error and need to wait until midnight UTC for your quota to reset. Consider upgrading to a higher tier if you frequently hit limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my Pro subscription?">
    Yes, manage your subscription directly through Polar.sh. Your Pro access will continue until the end of your billing period.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is usage tracked?">
    - **Anonymous**: By IP address
    - **Free/Pro**: By user ID (from your authenticated session)
    
    All tracking is stored in the `AnalysisRequest` table (see `schema.prisma:67`).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my Pro plan expires?">
    Your account automatically downgrades to the Free tier. Your analysis history is preserved, but Pro-only features (AI Insights, Data Flow) become locked.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
